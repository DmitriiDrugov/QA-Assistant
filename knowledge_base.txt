## 1) Password Reset (Self-Service)
Keywords: password, reset, credentials, account, portal, sspr
Summary: How users can reset their password using the self-service portal.

Instructions:
1. Open the company Self-Service Password Reset (SSPR) page.
2. Enter your company email and complete identity verification.
3. Choose a new password with at least 12 characters.
4. Wait a few minutes and sign in again.

Troubleshooting:
- If verification methods are missing, contact IT.
- If the account is locked, wait or contact IT.

## 2) VPN Setup (Windows & macOS)
Keywords: vpn, remote access, client, connection, certificate
Summary: How to install and connect to the corporate VPN.

Instructions:
1. Download the approved VPN client from the IT portal.
2. Install and open the VPN client.
3. Sign in with company credentials and approve MFA.
4. Confirm internal access.

## 2) Password Reset (Self-Service)
Keywords: password, reset, credentials, account, portal, sspr, login
Summary: How employees reset their Northbridge account password.

Instructions:
1. Open the Northbridge Self-Service Password Reset portal.
2. Enter your company email address.
3. Complete identity verification using MFA or backup email.
4. Choose a new password with at least 12 characters, one number, and one special character.
5. Wait up to 5 minutes for the new password to sync across company services.

Troubleshooting:
- If MFA is not configured, contact IT Operations.
- If the account is locked after 5 failed attempts, wait 15 minutes or open an IT ticket.

## 3) VPN Setup (Windows & macOS)
Keywords: vpn, remote access, client, connection, certificate, office, remote work
Summary: How employees connect to internal company systems from outside the office.

Instructions:
1. Download the approved Northbridge Secure Access client from the IT portal.
2. Install the client and sign in with your company email.
3. Approve the MFA prompt.
4. Select the "Corporate Network" profile.
5. Verify access to the intranet and internal dashboards.

Notes:
- VPN is required for finance systems, staging environments, and admin dashboards.
- Split tunneling is disabled on company-managed laptops.

## 4) New Employee Onboarding
Keywords: onboarding, new hire, employee, laptop, access, first day, account
Summary: Standard onboarding process for new employees.

Process:
1. HR creates the employee record in the People system.
2. IT Operations provisions email, SSO, laptop, and baseline software.
3. The hiring manager submits role-based access requests.
4. The employee completes security training in the first 3 business days.
5. Access to sensitive systems is granted only after manager approval.

Default equipment:
- Engineering: 16-inch laptop, external monitor, dock, security key
- Support and Sales: 14-inch laptop, headset, dock
- Finance and HR: 14-inch laptop, privacy screen, dock

## 6) Expense Reimbursement Policy
Keywords: expense, reimbursement, travel, receipt, finance, policy, approval
Summary: How employees submit business expenses.

Policy:
- Expenses must be submitted within 30 calendar days.
- Every expense requires a receipt or invoice.
- Manager approval is required before Finance review.
- Reimbursements are paid in the next monthly payroll cycle.

Allowed examples:
- Business travel
- Customer meeting meals
- Conference tickets
- Pre-approved software purchases

Not allowed without pre-approval:
- Personal electronics
- Home office furniture above the budget cap
- First-class travel

## 8) Security Rules
Keywords: security, policy, mfa, phishing, device, compliance, access
Summary: Core security requirements for all employees.

Rules:
- MFA is mandatory for all company accounts.
- Company data must be stored only in approved systems.
- Suspicious emails must be reported using the phishing-report button.

Incident reporting:
